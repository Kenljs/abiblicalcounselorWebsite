Commonly Asked Questions

What to expect

What type of counseling is offered?

Lou offers biblical and thoughtful counseling for challenges relating to marriage, premarital counseling, various addictions, phobia, fear, depression, anxiety, parenting, grief, aging concerns, and various other issues of life. Lou also brings his caring support to pastors and missionaries. God is all-wise and He has given us wise instruction and answers in the Bible that transcend other forms of counsel — our goal is to lovingly and prayerfully help people obtain those answers to live lives that please and honor God.

When and where does counseling take place?

Lou counsels in his Sayville office. Sessions are available by appointment Monday through Saturday, with daytime and evening hours available.

What does counseling cost?

Individual sessions are $95. Marriage and family sessions are $120. A sliding scale is available by request.

How long is a counseling session?

Generally 60–90 minutes.

How do I get started?

Call Lou at 631-368-1831 or email him at abiblicalcounselor@gmail.com to set up an appointment.
