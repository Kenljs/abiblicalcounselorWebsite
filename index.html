What hope is there for my problems and questions?

In today's complex world, God's practical counsel is desperately needed. There is tremendous hope in the fact that God has provided His Word, the Bible, to give answers for your life's problems and questions. God says He has provided everything necessary for a person to know Him and to live in a way that pleases Him.

Therefore, the one who seeks after and follows God's answers for life can have great hope, contentment, and direction to handle all the pressures of life.

2 Peter 1:3
